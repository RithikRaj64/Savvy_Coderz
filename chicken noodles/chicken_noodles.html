FUN FACTS

  • The Chinese actually made the first chicken noodle soup — which they referred to as noodles in broth rather than soup — centuries before Campbell's produced its ever popular version in that can.
  • By federal law, a noodle must contain 5.5 percent egg solids to be called a noodle. The first noodles ever consumed in space were instant ramen noodles. Ramen is the Japanese word for Chinese “lo mein.” There's a whole museum in Yokohama, Japan dedicated to Cup Noodles!!
