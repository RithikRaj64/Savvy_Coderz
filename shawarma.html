FUN FACTS

  • Shawarma is a tasty dish which belongs to the Middle Eastern cuisine.
  • Interestingly, its name originates from the Turkish word ‘çevirme’ meaning ‘turn’ in English.
  • It is served in round Arabic bread with meat and vegetables, with some spice or sauce of choice, in it. Normally, the meat can either be lamb, beef, turkey, or chicken. Shawarma can also be spelled chawarma, shwarma, shawerma, or shoarmais.
