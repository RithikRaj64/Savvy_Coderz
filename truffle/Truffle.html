FUN FACTS

  • Chocolate truffles were first created in France in 1895.The original chocolate truffles were a ball of chocolate ganache that was dusted with cocoa powder.
  • The term ‘chocolate truffle’ is derived from the edible tuber fungus known as a ‘truffle’, which shares a similar appearance to the confectionery.
